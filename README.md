uhfat at-Tullab · Effective on the date of first App Store release.

Summary
Tuhfat at-Tullab respects your privacy completely. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app works fully offline. Everything you do — your reading progress, quiz scores, bookmarks, and settings — stays on your device.

What we store, locally only
Chapters you have marked as read
Quiz and practice scores
Bookmarks of verses
Theme, font size, voice, recitation speed
Daily streak counter
This data lives only on your device. We have no access. Uninstalling the app removes all of it.

What we do not collect
No name, email, or account
No location, contacts, photos, mic, or camera
No advertising or analytics identifiers
No crash reports or telemetry
No usage analytics or tracking
Third-party services
The app does not embed any analytics, advertising, attribution, or crash-reporting SDKs. The only external resource it may load is a Google Fonts stylesheet (for the Amiri and Inter typefaces). With no internet connection, the app falls back to system fonts and works fully.

Audio
The Arabic recitation feature uses your device's built-in text-to-speech via AVSpeechSynthesis. Audio is generated entirely on-device. Nothing is sent to any server.

Children
The app does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children. It is suitable for all ages.
